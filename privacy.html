Privacy policy

Effective 16 July 2026. Written in plain English because the law is the law, but the words should not be.

Who runs Forge

Forge is a one-person studio that turns your Google Business Profile into a professional website for your local business. The website lives at this domain. Email hello@forge.local and you reach the studio directly — there is no support team, no ticket queue, and no auto-responder.

What this policy covers

This policy covers the four ways you can interact with Forge through this site:

  1. Browsing the pages (home, free 7-point audit, Operator prospectus, confirmation).
  2. Requesting a private preview of what Forge would build for your business.
  3. Requesting the free 7-point audit of your Google Business Profile.
  4. Asking about the Operator plan, which is ongoing management of your website after launch.

It also covers the join-the-waitlist form that appears on the home page when the monthly launch cap is full.

What we collect

When you browse

No advertising cookies. No third-party trackers on this site. No selling or sharing of your visit data. Our hosting provider (Vercel) may log standard request metadata (IP address, user agent, request path) for security and uptime. Those logs are kept for a short, provider-defined window and are not joined with any form data you submit.

When you request a preview

  • The Google Business Profile link you paste.
  • Your WhatsApp number, if you choose to provide it. Marked optional. Used to reply faster.

That is the entire preview form. No name, email, or phone required.

When you request the free audit

  • Your name.
  • Your email address.
  • Your business name.
  • Your business category (one of: salon, clinic, cafe, studio, real estate, hotel, tuition, service, other).
  • The Google Business Profile link you want reviewed.
  • Your WhatsApp number, if you choose to provide it. Marked optional.

When you join the waitlist

  • Your name.
  • Your email address.
  • Your business category.
  • The Google Business Profile link you want a slot for.
  • Your WhatsApp number, if you choose to provide it. Marked optional.

When you ask about the Operator plan

  • Your email address.
  • Your WhatsApp number, if you choose to provide it. Marked optional.

What we do with it

We use the information exactly for what each form says it is for:

  • Previews. Read your Google Business Profile, build a private preview, send it within 24 hours.
  • Audits. Review the seven points listed on the audit page, write a private report, email it within 48 hours.
  • Waitlist. Hold your place in the queue, email you when a slot opens.
  • Operator enquiries. Reply within one business day with the right plan and the next slot.

That is the entire purpose. We do not enrich the data, score you, or retarget you.

How long we keep it

  • Preview requests. Kept until your preview is delivered, archived 90 days in case of follow-up, then deleted.
  • Audit requests. Kept 12 months so you can refer back, then deleted.
  • Waitlist entries. Kept 90 days. If you do not claim a slot, you are removed and would need to rejoin.
  • Operator enquiries. Kept 12 months, or for the duration of any active plan, whichever is longer.

Who we share it with

No one. Not for marketing. Not for resale. Not for any reason.

The two narrow exceptions:

  • Hosting and infrastructure. Vercel serves the site, and a form-handling service (Resend) receives form submissions and forwards them to email and CRM. Each of these services is contractually bound to protect the data they hold for us.
  • The law. If a court order or equivalent legal obligation requires disclosure, we will comply, and we will tell you we did.

How we protect it

HTTPS in transit. Form submissions go to a single, audited provider. We do not store your Google Business Profile data on any system beyond what is needed to deliver the preview or audit.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time to:

  • Tell you exactly what we hold about you.
  • Correct it.
  • Delete it.

Email hello@forge.local and we will action the request within seven days. There is no automated portal, because this is a one-person studio and you should not have to log in to delete yourself.

Children

This site is not for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy

If we change anything material, we update the Effective date at the top of this page. The previous version remains available on request.

The short version. We collect what each form needs, use it for exactly what the form says, do not sell it, do not enrich it, do not retarget, and delete it on the schedule above. If you want your data gone, email and it is gone.

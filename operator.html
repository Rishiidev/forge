Honest scope

What is included, and what is not.

Good partnerships need clear edges. The Operator plan is for the running of the website. Larger work is a separate conversation, quoted before it starts.

Included

  • Hosting, SSL, backups, monitoring
  • Security and dependency updates
  • Content and photo changes within your plan's quota
  • Small design refinements within your plan's quota
  • Quarterly audit re-review (Active and Forged)
  • Priority response for breakage
  • Monthly summary
  • Domain renewal reminders

Not included

  • New pages, new sections, new features beyond the launched scope
  • Logo, brand identity, or large design work
  • Ad campaigns, paid media, social media management
  • Copywriting beyond light edits to existing pages
  • Photography or video production
  • Major replatforming or replatform migrations
  • Anything we did not agree to in writing
